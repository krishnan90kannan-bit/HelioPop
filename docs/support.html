What to include in your support request

  • Your device model, for example iPhone 15, iPad, or Android device.
  • Your operating system version, for example iOS 17 or Android 15.
  • The HelioPop app version, if available.
  • A short description of the issue and the steps that caused it.
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if it helps explain the problem.

Common help topics

Sound or music is not playing

  • Check that your device volume is turned up.
  • Check the app settings for music and sound effects.
  • Close and reopen the app if audio does not start after changing settings.

Ads are not loading

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Ads may not be available every time. Please try again later.

2-player Wi-Fi mode

  • Both devices must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
  • On iOS, allow local network permission if prompted.
  • Make sure both players are using the same app version when possible.

Progress, scores, and achievements

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device.
  • Deleting the app or clearing app data may reset progress.
